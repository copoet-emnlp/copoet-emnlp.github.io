The castle walls. i pressed my forehead against the window.

The castle walls. i’ve never seen a castle.

In the castle, he snatched a hare.

The castle looms large and drear.

And i, the gate of this fortified castle.

In that far land beyond the sea. o thou that dost appear.

The sand of the dream is keeping time, trembling, clear.

And the sand where no one treads is whiter than snow.

In the land of never, never i'll be able to hear.

And the land as a whole is breathing despair.

The kingdom of the dead, which i retell.

To this kingdom i’ve come to bring you.

I will take my kingdom to the lions.

To be the king of this world.

I've come to offer you my kingdom.

None of the above

 
 
And i, a serf of the king.

The king of hearts, the lord of all hearts.

The king of hearts, the lord of hosts, is dead.

The king of hearts, the lord of all weathers.

The king sat at the table calm.

None of the above

 
 
The people of America are fed up,

The kings of old are crying,

I don't know what I'm feeling, but I know I feel a lot.

The people of the land are all frightened,

And the stifling heat reaches to the sky

None of the above

 
 
And the king, who at that time was lying.

The king of diamonds: unhappy in lying.

Where is my king? — where is he lying.

The king, in his state apartment lying.
