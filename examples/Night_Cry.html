I am the sky. i am fire in the night.

no bird has heart to speak aloud. here in a world without а sky.

And the night wind carries it high.

The ray of a star, falling on the night sky, would pierce the eye.

And if at night a thief should fly.

The equator, and the night is your eye.

Then i asked the stars, why.

To the stars, your eyes still supply.

The stars, the moon and stars again i ask why.

The stars, the stars! ah, why.

The stars are in the sky, that’s why.
