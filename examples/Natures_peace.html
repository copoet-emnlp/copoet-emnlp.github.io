I saw tyger, tigger climbing up the mountains.

The mountains’ vigil, the night’s final bow.

Then the mountains shook, and all the earth.

The world was young, the mountains green.

The mountains, the deserts, and the sea.

None of the above

 
 
Nature is like a naughty child

Nature is like a savage lioness

Nature is like a tangled web.

Nature is like a prank

Nature is like a tangle of weeds

None of the above

 
 
Taking cares away is a good thing.

Taking cares away is a great thing.

Taking cares away is a mighty thing.

Taking cares away is a bliss.

Taking cares away is like a lullaby.

None of the above

 
 
The trees they had one large bird body that was.

And a lighthouse flashes above the trees.

In a corner among hemp dust and almond trees.

The fir-trees' bending all together.

The trees and flowers are all ablaze.

None of the above
