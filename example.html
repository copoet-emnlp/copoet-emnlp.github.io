Pivoting Our Destiny

Poem Draft.

Instructions

I'm not looking for a destiny.
But a better way out.
Destiny is a strange term
To describe what life is all about.
Life is meaningful only in the moment you live it.
So why hold onto grudges that make it hard to pivot
But the truth is all about making sense of who we are.
Cherish each moment and then you'll go really far.
